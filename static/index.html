THE PIPELINE

Every incident follows the same path. The question is: who's reading it — a human at 3AM, or an AI that classified it in 2 seconds?

STEP 01

Incident

Prometheus alert. PagerDuty webhook. Log anomaly.

STEP 02

Classify

LLM analyzes context. Category: infra / app / security / deps.

STEP 03

Score

Severity P1-P4. Confidence score. Impact radius estimation.

STEP 04

Route

Telegram → on-call team. Slack → #incidents. Email → management.

STEP 05

Auto-Fix

Known remediation? Execute. Unknown? Human-in-the-loop. Learn.
